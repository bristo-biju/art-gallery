Cattleya Orchid and Three Hummingbirds

Martin Johnson Heade — c.1871

Heade's lush still life combines exotic orchids and iridescent hummingbirds in a tropical setting. The painting's detail and vibrant color reflect Heade's fascination with nature and scientific observation.

Movement & influence: American Luminism / Naturalism. Heade's work bridges scientific illustration and fine art, influencing later botanical and wildlife artists.
