Les Demoiselles d'Avignon

Pablo Picasso — 1907

Les Demoiselles d'Avignon is a radical proto-Cubist work that breaks figures into angular, fragmented planes. Picasso challenged traditional perspective and representation.

Visual accents use geometric color blocks to echo Cubism's faceted planes.
