The Persistence of Memory

Salvador Dalí — 1931

Dalí's iconic image of melting clocks blends hyper-realistic detail with dreamlike distortions. The work explores subconscious time, memory, and the instability of reality.

Page accents use surreal, slightly desaturated tones to place emphasis on uncanny imagery.
