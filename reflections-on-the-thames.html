Reflections on the Thames, Westminster

John Atkinson Grimshaw — c.1880

Grimshaw's atmospheric cityscape glows with moonlight and mist, capturing the quiet poetry of Victorian London. His mastery of light and reflection creates a dreamlike, almost photographic effect.

Movement & influence: Victorian Realism / Aestheticism. Grimshaw's nocturnes influenced later urban landscape painting and photography.
