The Jack Pine

Tom Thomson — 1916

Thomson's iconic Canadian landscape features a solitary pine silhouetted against a northern lake. The bold colors and expressive brushwork celebrate the rugged beauty of the wilderness.

Movement & influence: Group of Seven / Canadian Modernism. Thomson's work inspired a generation of Canadian artists to explore national identity through landscape.
